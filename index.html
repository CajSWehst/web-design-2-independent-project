Type of Work

At RookaMan Designs we take on a wide variety of different projects so we can best suit your needs and create a solution for your project. Our services include and are not limited to:

Marketing & Branding

RookaMan Designs provides marketing and branding consultation and services in both print and digital media. We cater to the design needs of the client so our services can create their specific brand. RookaMan Designs have past experiences in campaign, branding, and logo design, we also offer event planning and coordination.

Illustration

Illustrations are one of our specialties. We offer unique hand-drawn commissions for our clients that will give your brand a unique look. This service can be implemented on t-shirts, business cards, posters, product packaging, and much more.

Motion Design

Motion graphic services are offered for those who want an interactive outlook on their brand. From spicing up a website with motion design to creating an interactive logo for your media advertisements. We implement motion design on a wide variety of platforms such as social media, web pages, and even marketing ads.

Web Design

Our web design services produce stunning websites that are accessible, unique, and will grab your viewers’ attention. Our web design team focuses your brand on interactive websites and apps to increase user traffic to get your message out there.
